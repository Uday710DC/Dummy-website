Who Is This Study For?

Is this the right study for you?

This study is for adults who have been told they have lichen planus on their skin, and whose rash is itchy and hasn't cleared with creams. Here are some simple guidelines:

✓

You are 18 years old or older

Any adult can apply. There is no maximum age, everyone is welcome to check.

✓

A doctor diagnosed skin lichen planus

A healthcare provider has confirmed lichen planus on your skin, not another kind of rash.

✓

The rash is itchy, moderate to severe

Your rash is bothersome and the itch is hard to ignore, rather than mild.

✓

Creams tried for months haven't cleared it

You have used a prescription cream or ointment for at least three months without it clearing.

✕

Not pregnant or breastfeeding

Because this study uses an investigational medicine, it is not suitable during pregnancy or breastfeeding.

✕

No prior IL-15 biologic treatment

If you have taken a biologic that targets IL-15 before, this study would not be a match. Most people have not.

💰

Your time can be reimbursed

Taking part costs you nothing. All study-related medical care, tests, and visits are fully covered by Novartis. Qualified participants may also be reimbursed for their time and travel over the course of the study.
